About Us

Foundation for Study of Indian Culture

The Foundation for Study of Indian Culture (FSIC) is a research centre dedicated to fostering original, critical, and rigorous inquiry into Indian traditions. It provides a space for young scholars to engage deeply with cultural, philosophical, and intellectual legacies of Bharat, drawing from both textual and lived experiences.

  • Research rooted in Indian knowledge systems
  • Reimagining social sciences through Adhyatma

Reclaiming Indian narratives through rigorous inquiry

FSIC conducts interdisciplinary research rooted in Indian epistemologies to challenge colonial frameworks and develop alternative social science perspectives.

  • Focus on Adhyatma and cultural difference
  • Builds Indian theories in social sciences and humanities
  • Encourages context-sensitive scholarship
