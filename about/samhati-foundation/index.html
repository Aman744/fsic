About Us

Samhati Foundation

Samhati Foundation initiated the research division called Foundation for Study of Indian Culture (FSIC) in 2025. Samhati Foundation is broadly dedicated to nurturing and strengthening the Indian family system, which has long been the cornerstone of social and cultural life in the country. The organization believes that the values embedded in traditional family structures—such as mutual respect, responsibility, care, and intergenerational bonding—are vital for fostering resilience and harmony in society. By highlighting the relevance of these values in contemporary contexts, Samhati Foundation works to bridge the wisdom of tradition with the realities of modern life. A central focus of the Foundation is engaging with the youth, who often navigate rapidly changing social and cultural environments. Through workshops, awareness programs, and interactive initiatives, Samhati Foundation seeks to help young people appreciate the significance of family as both a support system and a source of identity. By creating spaces for dialogue and reflection, the organization aims not only to raise awareness but also to inspire the next generation to carry forward and reimagine the strengths of the Indian family system in ways that remain meaningful for the future.
